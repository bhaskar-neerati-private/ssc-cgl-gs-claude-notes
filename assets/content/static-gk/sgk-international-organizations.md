# International Organisations & Their Headquarters

## The Building That Represents Humanity

On October 24, 1945, 51 nations signed the United Nations Charter in San Francisco. The choice of a permanent headquarters became a geopolitical battle. The Soviet Union wanted it in Europe; China wanted it in Asia; Britain wanted it in London. In the end, John D. Rockefeller Jr. donated $8.5 million to purchase 18 acres of Manhattan waterfront in New York City. The building was completed in 1952 — a glass-and-steel tower rising over the East River, technically international territory, not subject to US law. Today, 193 nations are represented there, more than existed as independent states in 1945. The headquarters question in SSC exams is simple but the answer is not always obvious: Geneva houses more UN bodies than New York, but New York hosts the UN General Assembly. Get the distinction right.

## Exam Traps — 16 Things SSC Will Test

1. **UNEP HQ = Nairobi, Kenya** — only major UN body headquartered in Africa; often confused with Geneva or New York.
2. **FAO, WFP, IFAD**: all three food-related bodies are headquartered in **Rome, Italy**.
3. **ILO** (International Labour Organisation) = oldest UN specialised agency (founded **1919**, before UN itself). HQ = Geneva.
4. **ICJ** (International Court of Justice) vs **ICC** (International Criminal Court): both in **The Hague**, Netherlands, but different functions — ICJ settles disputes between states; ICC prosecutes individuals for war crimes.
5. **WTO** replaced **GATT** in **1995**; HQ = Geneva. GATT was not an organisation but an agreement.
6. **ADB** = Manila, Philippines. India = largest borrower from ADB (historically).
7. **AIIB** = Beijing, China; launched **2016**; China-led; India is the second largest shareholder.
8. **NDB** (New Development Bank) = BRICS bank = **Shanghai, China**; not Beijing.
9. **OPEC** = Vienna, Austria (not Saudi Arabia; OPEC Secretariat is in Vienna though most members are in Middle East).
10. **SCO** = India became full member in **2017** (along with Pakistan). HQ = Beijing.
11. **NATO** HQ = **Brussels, Belgium** (not Washington DC where the major decisions involve US, but HQ is Brussels).
12. **IOC** = **Lausanne, Switzerland** (not Geneva — both are in Switzerland but different cities).
13. **ICC** (Cricket) = **Dubai, UAE** — different from ICC (International Criminal Court) in The Hague. SSC loves this confusion.
14. **SAARC** = Kathmandu, Nepal; 8 members (India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Nepal, Bhutan, Maldives, Afghanistan).
15. **G20 added African Union** in **2023** (India's G20 presidency) — AU became the 21st member (the 20 + EU + AU).
16. **WHO founded April 7, 1948** = reason for World Health Day being on April 7.
