# India's Firsts — Politics, Services, Science, Sports & Women

## The Woman Who Said No to a President

In 1966, when Lal Bahadur Shastri died suddenly in Tashkent, the Congress party needed a new Prime Minister quickly. Party bosses expected K. Kamaraj to manage a compromise. Instead, they turned to Indira Gandhi — Nehru's daughter, then a relatively inexperienced minister. Morarji Desai, the leading candidate, confidently predicted she would be a rubber stamp. "A dumb doll," one newspaper called her. Within two years, she had outmanoeuvred every senior Congress leader, won a massive election victory on her own mandate ("Garibi Hatao"), nationalized fourteen banks, and split the Congress party. India's first woman Prime Minister turned out to be its most dominant political personality since independence. The "firsts" in Indian history are not just trivia — they mark the moments when impossible things became inevitable.

## Exam Traps — 16 Things SSC Will Test

1. **First President** = Rajendra Prasad (only 2-term President); **First Woman President** = Pratibha Patil (12th President).
2. **Droupadi Murmu** = **first tribal President** of India (2022, 15th President) — distinct from first woman (Pratibha Patil).
3. **First Woman PM** = Indira Gandhi (1966); **First non-Congress PM** = Morarji Desai (1977).
4. **First PM from Gujarat** = Morarji Desai; **First PM born after Independence** = Narendra Modi.
5. **Rakesh Sharma** = first Indian in space (1984); said **"Saare Jahan se Accha"** when asked about India from space.
6. **Kalpana Chawla** = first Indian-American woman in space; died in **Columbia shuttle disaster (2003)**, not first mission.
7. **Aryabhata** (1975) = first Indian satellite; launched by **Soviet Union** (not ISRO's own launch vehicle).
8. **Chandrayaan-3** landed on **August 23, 2023** — South Polar region; now celebrated as **National Space Day**.
9. **Abhinav Bindra** = first individual Olympic gold for India (2008, 10m air rifle); **first Olympic medal ever** = K. D. Jadhav (1952 wrestling bronze).
10. **First Olympic GOLD for India** = Men's Hockey team at **Amsterdam 1928** — team sport, not individual.
11. **First Chief Justice of India** = H. J. Kania (Harilal Jekisundas Kania) — SSC tests full name.
12. **First Speaker of Lok Sabha** = G. V. Mavalankar; **First Woman Speaker** = Meira Kumar (2009).
13. **Satyendranath Tagore** (Rabindranath's elder brother) = first Indian to join ICS (1863); SSC tests this Tagore vs Tagore confusion.
14. **First Indian Film** = Raja Harishchandra (1913, silent) by **Dada Saheb Phalke**; **First talkie** = Alam Ara (1931).
15. **First woman IPS officer** = Kiran Bedi (1972); Ramon Magsaysay award winner; first Director General of Bureau of Narcotics.
16. **First Chief Minister** (woman) = Sucheta Kripalani (1963, UP); first woman Governor = Sarojini Naidu (1947, UP).
