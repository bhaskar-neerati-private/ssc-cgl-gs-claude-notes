# India Superlatives — Largest, Longest, Highest & Records

## The Bridge Over the Deepest Gorge

In 2017, the Chenab Railway Bridge was under construction over the Chenab River gorge in Jammu & Kashmir — a bridge so high that when complete, it would stand taller than the Eiffel Tower. At 359 metres above the river valley floor, it became the world's highest railway bridge when it was completed in 2022. The engineering challenge was staggering: the bridge needed to withstand 260 km/h winds, be earthquake-proof up to 8 on the Richter scale, and support fully loaded trains. The workers who built it were suspended over a chasm during construction. India — a country where three decades ago many major rivers had no permanent bridges — now holds world records in bridge engineering. Superlatives like this are not just trivia; they map India's development trajectory. SSC tests them every single year.

## Exam Traps — 16 Things SSC Will Test

1. **Largest state by area** = **Rajasthan** (not Madhya Pradesh — MP was largest until 2000 when Chhattisgarh was carved out, and Rajasthan has always been larger in area; MP is largest by forest area).
2. **Largest national park** = **Hemis** (Ladakh) — NOT Jim Corbett; Jim Corbett = oldest national park (1936).
3. **Chilika Lake** = largest **coastal lagoon** in Asia; Wular Lake = largest **freshwater lake** in India — these are tested together.
4. **Highest dam** = **Tehri Dam** (260.5 m, Uttarakhand); **Longest dam** = **Hirakud Dam** (~26 km, Odisha).
5. **Longest road bridge** = **Bhupen Hazarika Setu** (Dhola-Sadiya Bridge) = **9.15 km** over Lohit River, Assam.
6. **Highest railway bridge** = **Chenab Bridge** (359 m) = J&K; world's highest railway bridge — not Pamban Bridge.
7. **Longest National Highway** = **NH-44** (Srinagar to Kanyakumari; ~3,745 km) — old numbering was NH-7.
8. **Atal Tunnel** = world's longest highway tunnel above 10,000 feet; **9.02 km**; Manali-Leh road; not to be confused with Pir Panjal (railway tunnel).
9. **Wettest place in India** = **Mawsynram** (Meghalaya) — NOT Cherrapunji (Cherrapunji held the record earlier; both in Meghalaya).
10. **Statue of Unity** = **182 m** = world's tallest statue; dedicated to **Sardar Patel**; Gujarat; inaugurated October 31, 2018.
11. **India overtook China** in population in **2023** (approximately April 2023) — India is now world's most populous country.
12. **India's GDP rank** = 5th (Nominal) and 3rd (PPP) — these are different measures and SSC asks both.
13. **Largest cricket stadium** = **Narendra Modi Stadium** (Motera), Ahmedabad — 1,32,000 capacity; renamed from Sardar Patel Stadium.
14. **Majuli** = world's largest river island (Brahmaputra, Assam) — SSC may compare with Sriharikota (island but launch site, not largest).
15. **Atal Setu** = Mumbai Trans Harbour Link = **21.8 km** = India's longest sea bridge; opened **January 2024**.
16. **Sambhar Lake** = largest **salt water lake** in India (Rajasthan); **Chilika** = brackish coastal lagoon; **Wular** = freshwater lake — three different categories.
