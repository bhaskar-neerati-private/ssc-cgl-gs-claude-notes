# Abbreviations, Acronyms & Full Forms — Complete SSC Reference

## The Full Form That Changed Everything

In 1991, the Finance Minister of India stood up in Parliament and presented a Budget that included something called "LPG Reforms." Not cooking gas — but Liberalisation, Privatisation, and Globalisation. The man was Dr. Manmohan Singh; the PM was P. V. Narasimha Rao; the year was 1991; and the acronym LPG would be debated for the next three decades. India's economy had been closed since independence — foreign investment limited, industries state-controlled, markets protected. LPG opened all of that. A three-letter abbreviation — usually translated as cooking fuel — became shorthand for one of the most consequential policy shifts in modern Indian history. SSC loves acronyms. They test not just what the letters stand for, but which ministry runs the scheme, where the body is headquartered, and when it was established.

## Exam Traps — 16 Things SSC Will Test

1. **NITI Aayog** full form = **National Institution for Transforming India** (not "National Institute" — the word is "Institution"); replaced Planning Commission in **2015**.
2. **SEBI** = Securities and Exchange Board of India; established **1988** (as non-statutory); got statutory powers **1992** — SSC asks both.
3. **NABARD** = National Bank for **Agriculture and Rural Development** (not "National Agricultural Bank"); HQ = **Mumbai**.
4. **IRDAI** = Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of **India** (the "India" at the end is often tested); HQ = **Hyderabad** (not Mumbai).
5. **UNICEF** full form has "Fund" not "Foundation" — United Nations Children's **Fund**.
6. **IAEA** = International **Atomic** Energy Agency = Vienna, Austria (not Geneva).
7. **ICAO** = International Civil **Aviation** Organisation = **Montreal, Canada** (not USA or Europe).
8. **MUDRA** = Micro Units Development and Refinance Agency — under PMMY (Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana); not a bank itself but a refinance institution.
9. **CRR** (Cash Reserve Ratio) = deposits banks keep with **RBI** in CASH; **SLR** = deposits in liquid assets (gold + approved securities, not necessarily with RBI).
10. **NPA** = Non-Performing Asset = loan where EMI not paid for 90+ days = "bad loan."
11. **MGNREGS** has both "Act" (MGNREGA) and "Scheme" (MGNREGS) — the act creates the legal right; the scheme is the implementation.
12. **CPCB** = Central **Pollution** Control Board (not Conservation); **NTCA** = National **Tiger** Conservation Authority.
13. **NMC** (National Medical Commission) replaced **MCI** (Medical Council of India) in **2020** — SSC may still test MCI in historical contexts.
14. **UGC** = University **Grants** Commission (not "General" or "Graduate") — statutory body under UGC Act 1956.
15. **LPG** in economics context = Liberalisation, Privatisation, Globalisation (**1991** reforms). Do not confuse with Ujjwala's LPG (cooking gas).
16. **EXIM Bank** = Export-Import Bank of India (not "External Investment and Money Bank") — finances India's exports and outward investment.
