# Government Flagship Schemes & Social Programmes

## The Scheme That Put 400 Million People in Banks

On August 28, 2014, Prime Minister Narendra Modi launched the Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) with a single announcement: every household in India would get a bank account within 6 months, with a zero-balance requirement and a RuPay debit card. The target seemed impossible — India had over 40% of its population unbanked. Within the first week, 1.8 crore accounts were opened, setting a Guinness World Record. By 2024, over 53 crore (530 million) accounts had been opened under PMJDY. That one scheme — designed in a few months, implemented in a few weeks — brought more people into the formal banking system than all previous attempts combined over 67 years. SSC tests government schemes relentlessly: launch year, nodal ministry, target group, and the benefit provided.

## Exam Traps — 16 Things SSC Will Test

1. **PMJDY** launched **August 28, 2014** (not January 26 or Independence Day); zero-balance accounts; **RuPay card**.
2. **MUDRA tiers**: Shishu (up to ₹50,000), Kishor (₹50k–₹5 lakh), Tarun (₹5 lakh–₹10 lakh) — SSC tests these names.
3. **Ayushman Bharat**: ₹5 lakh/family/year health cover; launched **2018**; world's largest government health insurance scheme.
4. **Swachh Bharat Mission** launched on **October 2** (Gandhi Jayanti) **2014** — both urban and rural components.
5. **PM-KISAN**: ₹6,000/year in **three instalments of ₹2,000 each** — NOT a single payment.
6. **PMFBY** (crop insurance): Kharif premium = **2%**, Rabi = **1.5%**, Commercial crops = **5%**.
7. **Ujjwala Yojana** launched **May 1, 2016** at **Ballia, UP** — free LPG connections to BPL women.
8. **Beti Bachao Beti Padhao** launched **January 22, 2015** at **Panipat, Haryana** (where sex ratio was low).
9. **MGNREGS**: 100 days guaranteed employment; passed **2005**, implemented **2006** (not 2004 or 2007).
10. **Atal Pension Yojana**: Target group = unorganised sector workers; age **18–40**; pension at **60 years**.
11. **Sukanya Samriddhi**: deposits till girl turns **14**; matures when she turns **21**; for girl child only.
12. **NEP 2020** replaced the **1986 National Education Policy** — NOT the 1992 one.
13. **Make in India** = **September 25, 2014** (not August). **Startup India** = **January 16, 2016** (not 2014 or 2015).
14. **Smart Cities Mission**: **100** smart cities — not 50 or 75.
15. **PMGSY** (rural roads): habitations with **500+ population** (250+ in hilly/tribal/desert areas).
16. **Samagra Shiksha (2018)** merged three schemes: Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan + RMSA + Teacher Education — SSC asks what was merged.
